Job Prompt Studio (JPS) is an interface built for academic research and job-CV recommendation model training. This research is conducted at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU), and all collected data is used strictly for academic purposes.

Your participation helps strengthen machine learning models and contributes valuable insights toward developing improved job recommendation systems in the future.

Job Prompt Studio currently operates as a prototype and may return limited results. We invite you to enter a detailed job search prompt to help us better understand how users describe their career interests.

Thank you for participating and supporting this research.

Tip: be specific (industry, seniority, tools, outcomes). Keep it human.
