Job Prompt Studio (JPS) is a thesis prototype from the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) for research on AI-based job recommendation, CV understanding, and structured job-post normalization.

Use Candidate mode to get job recommendations from your prompt and CV, or use Employer / HR mode to store structured job posts in the research job bank.

Current coverage: candidate job search recommendations currently support Denmark only.

Uploaded CVs are processed through OCR and privacy redaction before parsing. Direct identifiers such as name, email, phone number, date of birth, address, and URLs are masked before AI-based extraction.

Tip: using both a role description and a CV usually improves recommendation quality.
