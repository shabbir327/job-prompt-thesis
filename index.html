Job Prompt Studio (JPS) is a thesis prototype from the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) for research on AI-based job recommendation and CV understanding.

You can either describe the kind of role you want or upload your CV. Your input helps improve future recommendation systems for academic research.

Accepted format: PDF only.

Tip: the more detail you provide, the better the recommendations and parsing quality.
