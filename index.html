Find career directions from your prompt and CV.

Job Prompt Studio (JPS) is a thesis prototype from the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) for research on AI-based job recommendation and CV understanding.

You can describe the role you want, upload your PDF CV, or use both together. Using both usually gives the best recommendation quality.

Current coverage: job search recommendations currently support Denmark and Germany only.

The system currently supports job search in Denmark and Germany only.

Only supported cities and regions are shown for the selected country.

Accepted format: PDF only. If uploaded, the CV will be OCR-processed, privacy-redacted, and parsed together with your input.

Uploaded CVs are processed through OCR and privacy redaction before parsing. Direct identifiers such as name, email, phone number, date of birth, address, and URLs are masked before AI-based extraction.

Tip: using both a role description and a CV usually improves recommendation quality.
